About WhiskerWatchersDB

The biochemistry laboratory are actively engaged in mouse-related experiments, necessitating the implementation of a robust database system for efficient data management. This database serves as a centralized platform to track and organize various aspects of mouse related information. It facilitates the identification of mating pairs, harems, and their respective breeding cages while recording crucial details such as the date of birth and sex for each mouse. Additionally, the database links mice to specific experiments and strains, allowing researchers to monitor their involvement and outcomes accurately. Furthermore, it maintains comprehensive records of deceased mice and associates experimental protocols with individual mice, ensuring thorough documentation of research procedures. Access to this database is restricted to authorized laboratory personnel, who are granted secure entry through password protection measures.

About Us
